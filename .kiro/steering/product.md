# Product Overview

**Wisdomia** is a content publishing platform focused on storytelling and editorial content. The platform features:

- **Content Management**: Article publishing with rich media support (images, videos)
- **User Authentication**: Multi-provider auth (Google, GitHub, credentials)
- **Admin Dashboard**: Content creation and management interface
- **Category System**: Organized content by topics (Politics, Mystery, Crime, History, News, Science)
- **Featured Content**: Hero sliders and curated article sections
- **Multi-language Support**: English and Bengali language options
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with dark/light theme support

The platform serves as a digital magazine with editorial focus, featuring long-form articles, multimedia content, and a clean, modern reading experience.

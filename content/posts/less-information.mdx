We are drowning in data but starving for wisdom. The information age has delivered on its promise of access, but failed on its promise of enlightenment. 

## The Signal to Noise Ratio
Every day, we consume the equivalent of 174 newspapers worth of information. Most of it is noise. 

> "Information is not knowledge. Knowledge is not wisdom. Wisdom is not truth." - Frank Zappa

### Curating Your Input
To reclaim our clarity, we must become ruthless curators of our own attention.
*   **Unsubscribe** from the mediocre.
*   **Deep Read** instead of doom scroll.
*   **Seek Timelessness** over timeliness.

True insight comes from connecting dots, not just collecting them.

Burnout is no longer treated as a personal weakness. Research now frames it as a systems problem where workload, autonomy, and recovery are misaligned.

## What Teams Get Wrong

Many organizations add wellness perks while ignoring planning culture. Back-to-back meetings, unclear ownership, and constant urgency quietly erode performance.

### Low-Cost Fixes That Scale

- Protect two meeting-free focus blocks per week
- Write decisions down to reduce repeat debates
- Track recovery metrics, not only output metrics

Sustainable work design improves both retention and product quality.
